about us

Reliable Electrical Solutions by Bind

Bind Arabian Development is a leading technology-driven smart facilities service provider offering complete Integrated Facilities Management solutions in the sultanate of Oman. Established in India in 2016, our expertise has grown eventually into a Total Asset Management company by providing bespoke solutions that create value and support our clients to meet their business goals. Bind Arabian Development has diversified its client portfolio from master developments to residential, industrial, and commercial developments in standalone and community projects. Bind provides cost-effective solutions in facilities services through continuous commitments. Our expertise includes hard and soft facilities management with innovative solutions tailored to support and meet the needs of our clients. We have developed a real-time performance management system to establish a quality culture and diversify our client base portfolio from master developments to industrial and commercial projects.

Our key focus is to develop a high standards Facilities Management regime and implement best practices and innovation. Bind is committed to achieving successful delivery of the Facilities Management and firmly believes that this will be realized through regular commitment. Bind is committed to providing quality service to our clients, and we believe in establishing long-term and mutually beneficial relationships by delivering excellence with time-bound and cost-effective solutions. We also offer our people diligent development and growth by working with us and providing them with a progressive professional and personal development ecosystem.
