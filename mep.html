MEP Services

Sanitary Systems

We offer comprehensive sanitary system solutions tailored to meet the hygienic and operational needs of residential, commercial, and industrial buildings. Our sanitary systems are designed with a focus on efficiency, durability, and environmental sustainability.

From advanced piping systems and water-saving fixtures to automated flushing and drainage solutions, we ensure proper sanitation and ease of maintenance. Our solutions comply with international standards and are suitable for hotels, hospitals, office spaces, and residential complexes.

We also provide customized installation and integration with smart building technologies to ensure enhanced water management and user comfort.

Drainage Systems

We specialize in the design and implementation of efficient drainage systems that ensure proper wastewater management and prevent water accumulation. Our solutions are engineered to support both gravity and pressurized flow systems, providing effective drainage in all types of infrastructures.

Whether it’s a commercial complex, residential development, or industrial facility, we deliver drainage systems that meet regulatory standards and withstand environmental challenges. Our scope includes stormwater drainage, sewage systems, manholes, gully traps, and proper ventilation arrangements.

Our team focuses on long-term reliability, easy maintenance, and integration with sustainable water reuse and treatment technologies to enhance overall environmental impact.

Water Supply

We provide comprehensive water supply system solutions that ensure a consistent, hygienic, and efficient distribution of water across all types of buildings and facilities. Our systems are engineered to meet the demands of domestic, commercial, and industrial applications with a focus on reliability and water conservation.

Our services include the design and installation of water storage tanks, pump systems, piping networks, and pressure-boosting units. We incorporate advanced control systems to regulate water pressure and flow, ensuring optimal performance and reduced energy consumption.

We also promote sustainable water supply practices through the integration of treated greywater reuse, rainwater harvesting systems, and smart monitoring solutions for leak detection and water usage optimization.

Control Systems

Our Control Systems solutions are designed to offer centralized management and automation of building services, improving energy efficiency, safety, and operational convenience. We implement intelligent control strategies that integrate HVAC, lighting, access, and other critical systems through a single platform.

With the help of programmable logic controllers (PLCs), building management systems (BMS), and IoT-based smart controllers, we provide real-time monitoring and automation capabilities. These systems ensure precise control, predictive maintenance, and performance analytics to reduce costs and enhance productivity.

Whether it's for industrial plants, commercial complexes, or smart buildings, our customized control solutions are tailored to meet your specific operational goals and compliance requirements.

Lighting Systems

We offer advanced lighting system solutions that enhance the functionality, aesthetics, and energy efficiency of indoor and outdoor environments. Our expertise includes the design, supply, and installation of smart and conventional lighting systems tailored to residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.

Our solutions include LED lighting, motion-sensor lights, daylight harvesting systems, emergency and exit lighting, façade illumination, and architectural lighting. We focus on delivering sustainable and automated lighting setups that reduce energy consumption and align with green building standards.

Integrated with modern control systems, our lighting solutions offer scheduling, dimming, occupancy detection, and remote monitoring — ensuring maximum comfort and long-term savings.

Power Distribution

We deliver reliable and efficient power distribution solutions tailored to meet the energy demands of residential, commercial, and industrial infrastructures. Our systems ensure safe and seamless delivery of electrical power from the main source to end-user points with minimal loss and maximum control.

Our scope includes the supply and installation of main distribution boards (MDBs), sub-main distribution boards (SMDBs), final distribution boards (FDBs), panel boards, circuit protection devices, and cabling works. All systems are designed in compliance with international electrical standards for safety and performance.

We also integrate advanced metering, load balancing, and energy monitoring tools that help clients manage their consumption more effectively and avoid unnecessary outages or overloads.

Fire protection Systems

We provide comprehensive fire protection system solutions designed to safeguard life, property, and critical infrastructure. Our systems are engineered in accordance with international fire safety codes and are suitable for all types of facilities, including residential, commercial, and industrial buildings.

Our services include the design, installation, and maintenance of fire alarm systems, fire sprinkler systems, fire suppression systems (gas-based or chemical), fire hydrants, hose reels, and extinguishers. All components are carefully selected for reliability, quick response, and durability.

We also integrate our fire systems with building management systems (BMS) and emergency response protocols to ensure coordinated action during fire events. Regular inspection and testing are provided to maintain compliance and system readiness.

Piping Systems

We deliver high-quality piping system solutions designed for efficiency, durability, and compliance with international standards. Our piping services cover a wide range of applications including water supply, drainage, fire protection, HVAC, and specialized industrial systems.

We work with various piping materials such as HDPE, PPR, PVC, GI, stainless steel, and copper to meet the specific needs of each project. Our installations are engineered for leak-free performance, easy maintenance, and long-term reliability under varying pressure and temperature conditions.

From design and material selection to fabrication and commissioning, our team ensures smooth execution and integration of piping systems within the broader MEP infrastructure.

HVAC Systems

We offer end-to-end HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) system solutions that ensure optimal indoor air quality, thermal comfort, and energy efficiency. Our services are tailored to meet the demands of residential, commercial, and industrial environments.

Our expertise covers chilled water systems, VRF/VRV systems, split units, air handling units (AHUs), fan coil units (FCUs), ducting works, ventilation systems, and exhaust setups. We prioritize eco-friendly and cost-effective solutions that comply with international energy standards.

From load calculations and system design to installation, testing, and commissioning — we deliver reliable HVAC systems that are scalable, low-maintenance, and integrated with smart control technologies for better performance monitoring and automation.
